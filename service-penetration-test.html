Penetration Test

A penetration test is the best way to identify vulnerabilities in your system. It involves testing the system to see if it can be breached or compromised in any way. The service can help you identify weaknesses in your system before they are exploited by attackers. We can give you peace of mind knowing that your system is secure, and it can also help you improve your security posture.

Since T-800 was founded in 2009, we have performed serveral of penetration test against all kinds of application and online services. Our service inclued black-box penetration test, white-box test and code audits. Many languages are used in programming and therefor we offer this service for many of them. From languages as PHP, JavaScript, ActionScript, Java, Ruby, Python and Perl to c++ and Delphin. We value thorough tests, human interaction and communication and a short yet to-the-point report.
